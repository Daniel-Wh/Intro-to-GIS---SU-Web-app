How to use this site

Welcome to Introduction to Environmental GIS! This site is meant to be used as a tool for you during this course. We suggest you go through the contents of this page to get familiar with the course. As you proceed during the course, refer to the tutorial videos on how to use the various tools you'll be using in ArcMap. The data tab will be a repository for you and your classmates' as final projects come into view. The practive section should help you solidify some of ideas that will be covered this semester.

As you go through the labs in this course, continue to come back to this page for tutorials on the tools that you'll be using. If the tutorial does not answer your question, please reach out to a T/A.
