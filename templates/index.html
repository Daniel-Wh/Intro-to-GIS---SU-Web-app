    How to practice

    Getting familiar with operating any new software product requires practice. We've included previous labs for additional experience. If you decide to work through the labs, feel free to reach out to a T/A for feedback or questions. In addition to the labs, below are some helpful links to provide additional resources on important topics you will cover this semester.

    Mark Your Home

    This lab is great for practicing connecting to a folder, adding a shapefile to your map in arcmap, and seeing some of the tools you can use later

    Flood risk zone

    This lab is administered by ESRI and is a great lab to reinforce what you've learned within the first month or so of the course and give you an opportunity to play with some advanced tools.

    TX Elections

    This is the lab we used for some of the tutorials. It's great practice for data cleaning, joining data to a shapefile, and applying colors to the results
